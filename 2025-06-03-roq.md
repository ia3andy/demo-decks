##  SSG?

Ideal for scenarios with high read demands and relatively infrequent writes:
- [~]**Portfolio & Blogs**
- [~]**Personal website/Resume**
- [~]**Events/Catalog repository**
- [~]**Landing & corporate**

##  What is Roq?

```js
SSG + Quarkus super-powers = Roq
```

Following conventions from popular SSGs

(Jekyll, Hugo, ...)

```shell

quarkus run
```
